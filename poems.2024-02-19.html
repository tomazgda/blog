Something curls knots deep within…

2024-02-19


Something curls knots deep within,
as I tred unknown familiar places.
Impressed shivers scatter my skin,
as I alone pick out outward faces.

Somehow I balance, awkward, unsteady.
Amidst sound and life and laughter
I breathe, prepare, and once more I'm ready;
do they see my cracks as I faulter?

Somebody said to look after help,
Somebody who's words speak straight to me.
Upon the brink of change I think
I am now and want to be.
