Time is free to work woe or wonder…

2024-02-27

Time is free to work woe or wonder.
Fleeting time stirs minds insane.
Stretching time compels creation.
Frozen time reflects love or pain.

A deity is time in essence,
yet trivial is time's presence:
I need… I've got…
I've made… time tangible to us.

It is time that we strive for;
time we lust and crave.
So ashamed of our time wasted,
lest time take our world away.
